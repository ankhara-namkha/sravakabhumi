Sravakabhumi: The Hearer's Ground

From the Yogacarabhumi-sastra of Asanga
Fascicles 26–33
Translated from the Chinese of Xuanzang (T1579)

The first complete English translation of the Sravakabhumi, Asanga's comprehensive treatise on the hearer's path of meditation and realization. This translation covers fascicles 26 through 33 of Xuanzang's Chinese rendering of the Yogacarabhumi-sastra, encompassing the Second, Third, and Fourth Yoga Places.
