Glossary of Technical Terms

This glossary covers the principal Sanskrit, Chinese, and English technical terms used throughout the translation of the Sravakabhumi. Sanskrit is used as the canonical reference form, following the convention of the Nalanda Translation Committee (NTC). Well-established Sanskrit terms (shamatha, vipashyana, samadhi, klesha, etc.) are preserved in the running translation; less familiar terms are given English renderings with Sanskrit provided in parentheses at first use in each fascicle. The Chinese column gives Xuanzang's Classical Chinese rendering. Entries are listed alphabetically by Sanskrit term; compounds and derived forms appear under their root where possible.
