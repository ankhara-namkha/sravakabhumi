## CRITICAL FLAGS

### FLAG C-1: Rendering Mismatch — `prthag-jana` vs. `prithagjana`
- **Location:** Line 406
- **Text:** "An ordinary person (*prthag-jana*) of trainable nature..."
- **Glossary entry:** `凡夫/異生 | prithagjana | ordinary being(s) | ordinary beings`
- **Issues:**
  - (2) Sanskrit romanization differs: text uses `prthag-jana` (hyphenated); glossary uses `prithagjana` (no hyphen, different vowel)
  - (2) English rendering differs: text uses "ordinary person" but glossary specifies "ordinary being(s)" as the rendering
- **Decision required:** Either update glossary or correct translation. The transliteration discrepancy (`prthag-jana` vs. `prithagjana`) also needs standardization.

### FLAG C-2: Terms Not in Glossary — `sugata` and the phrase "well-gone ones"
- **Location:** Line 402
- **Text:** "The well-gone ones (*sugata*)"
- **Flag (1):** `sugata` does not appear in `MASTER_GLOSSARY.md`. Neither "well-gone" nor "well-gone ones" is present.
- **Note:** The term appears as an epithet of the Buddha (those who have permanently exhausted greed, aversion, and delusion). It is a significant technical term that should be registered.
- **Action needed:** Add `sugata / 善逝` to glossary with rendering and preferred-in-text form.

### FLAG C-3: Term Not in Glossary — `bodhisattva`
- **Location:** Line 404
- **Text:** "tathagatas and bodhisattvas"
- **Flag (1):** `bodhisattva` (菩薩) does not appear in `MASTER_GLOSSARY.md`.
- **Note:** `tathagata` (如來) is in the glossary (line 76). `bodhisattva` is a very commonly used term here and needs a glossary entry.
- **Action needed:** Add entry for 菩薩 / bodhisattva.

### FLAG C-4: Term Not in Glossary — `pancha-anantarya-karma`
- **Location:** Line 366
- **Text:** "the five acts of immediate retribution (*pancha-anantarya-karma*)"
- **Flag (1):** `pancha-anantarya-karma` / "five acts of immediate retribution" does not appear in `MASTER_GLOSSARY.md`.
- **Note:** The glossary entry for `karma-avarana` (line 137) references "Five heinous deeds" but the term `pancha-anantarya-karma` itself is not registered.
- **Action needed:** Add glossary entry for 五無間業 / pancha-anantarya-karma / "five acts of immediate retribution" (or "five heinous deeds" — terminology needs to be standardized).

### FLAG m-2: "Warmth of wholesome dharmas" — partially in glossary
- **Location:** Line 406
- **Text:** "the warmth of wholesome dharmas"

- **Status:** Minor — rendering is contextually consistent; no action required unless the editor wants standardization.

### FLAG m-3: "Shramana adornments" — not in glossary

- **Text:** "the shramana adornments previously described"
- **Flag (1):** "Shramana adornments" (沙門莊嚴) is not in the glossary.

### FLAG m-4: "Resolute conviction" — not in glossary

- **Action needed:** Add 勝解 / adhimoksha / "resolute conviction" (or verify against reference library for preferred rendering).

### FLAG m-5: "Pure faith" — not formally registered

- **Action needed:** Check whether 淨信 / prasada should be distinguished from 信 / shraddha in the glossary.
