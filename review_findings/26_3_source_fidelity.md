### FINDING 2 — ACCURACY CHECK: Opening frame (T30.0425b20)

**Chinese:** 云何建立補特伽羅。謂由十一差別道理。應知建立補特伽羅。云何十一差別道理。一根差別故。二眾差別故。三行差別故。四願差別故。五行迹差別故。六道果差別故。七加行差別故。八定差別故。九生差別故。十退不退差別故。十一障差別故。

**English:** "How are persons established? It is by eleven criteria of differentiation that persons should be understood as established. What are the eleven criteria of differentiation?" [followed by numbered list 1-11]

**Analysis:** 差別道理 is rendered "criteria of differentiation." The word 道理 (reasoning, principle, rationale) is rendered as "criteria," which is slightly loose. A more literal rendering would be "principles of differentiation" or "bases of differentiation." However, "criteria" is defensible as it captures the functional meaning — these are the principles by which classification proceeds.

**Verdict:** Minor nuance. Not a mistranslation. **No issue flagged.**
