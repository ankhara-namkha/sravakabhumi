## Overall Assessment

The translation of sections 31.8–31.11 is highly faithful to the Chinese source. All structural divisions match. All major doctrinal content is present. The two moderate issues are: (1) 不修加行 rendered as "insufficient effort" rather than "not practicing" — a meaningful difference since the antidote differs from that for excessive effort; and (2) the 熱/厭 (urgency/disenchantment) formula compressed to only "disenchantment," losing the samvega dimension. The seven minor issues are mostly acceptable compressions of formulaic closing sentences and small omissions. No critical omissions or mistranslations found.
