### Section 30.7 — Guarding the Samadhi Resources [0449c15 cont.]

**S-03. CLEAN.** 謂若成就戒律儀者。即於是處為令不退住不放逸 = "One who has achieved the discipline of moral conduct dwells in non-heedlessness to prevent regression." Accurate.

**S-04. CLEAN.** 如佛所誡如佛所許。圓滿戒蘊學處差別。精進修行常無懈廢 = "As the Buddha has instructed and permitted, one perfects the points of training within the aggregate of discipline, practicing diligently and never slackening." Accurate.

**S-05. CLEAN.** 如是能於己所證得尸羅相應學道無退。亦能證得先所未證尸羅相應殊勝學道 = "one does not regress from the path of training in moral conduct already attained, and one also attains superior paths of training not yet realized." Accurate.

**S-06. CLEAN.** 如說成就戒律儀。如是成就根律儀。於食知量。初夜後夜覺寤瑜伽正知而住 = "so too with sense restraint, moderation in food, wakefulness through the first and last watches of the night, alertness in one's activities." Accurate. The Chinese lists these as parallel to shila-samvara, and the English correctly parallels them with "As with moral conduct, so too with..."

**S-07. CLEAN.** 如是乃至成就所有沙門莊嚴 = "and all the shramana adornments." Accurate.

**S-08. (m) Minor compression — no content loss.** Chinese: 隨所獲得資糧所攝善法差別。皆能防護令不退失 = "Whatever wholesome dharmas pertaining to the samadhi resources one has already obtained, one protects them against regression." Accurate.

**S-09. (m) Minor note.** Chinese: 為速圓滿為如所說無增無減。平等現行 = "to bring them to swift and complete fulfillment -- neither adding to nor diminishing from what has been taught." The phrase 平等現行 (equally/evenly manifest/practice) is compressed into the overall sentence rather than rendered independently. The sense is preserved through the phrase structure, but 平等現行 could be made more explicit (e.g., "practicing in even accord with what has been taught").
**Severity: m**

**S-10. CLEAN.** 發生樂欲增上欣慕。恒常安住勇猛精進 = "one generates the aspiration and heightened enthusiasm... and one dwells with constant, vigorous diligence." Accurate.

**S-11. CLEAN.** 如是遠離順退分法。修習能順勝分法時樂住遠離 = "Having thus guarded against factors conducive to regression and cultivated factors conducive to progress, one delights in seclusion." Accurate. This transitional sentence linking 30.7 to 30.8 is correctly placed and translated.

### Section 30.8 — Seclusion [0450a03]

**S-12. CLEAN.** 云何遠離。謂處所圓滿。威儀圓滿。遠離圓滿。是名遠離 = "What is seclusion? It has three aspects: perfection of place, perfection of deportment, and perfection of seclusion proper." Accurate. The Chinese 遠離圓滿 is well rendered as "perfection of seclusion proper" to distinguish it from the overarching topic.

**S-13. CLEAN.** The three types of dwelling places are correctly identified:
- 空閑室 (mountain caves, straw-thatched huts) = "empty rooms"
- 林樹下 (great forest) = "tree-base"
- 阿練若 (remote burial grounds, border regions) = "forest dwelling"
All three correctly mapped to their definitions.

**S-14. (M) Mistranslation — 稻秆積.** Chinese: 山谷巖穴稻秆積等名空閑室 = "Mountain caves, straw-stacked huts, and the like are called empty rooms." The translation renders 稻秆積 as "straw-thatched huts." But 稻秆積 literally means "heaps/stacks of rice-straw" — referring to shelters made by piling rice straw, not proper thatched huts. This is a minor inaccuracy in the image conveyed. "Straw-pile shelters" or "rice-straw stacks" would be more precise.
**Severity: M** (mistranslation of a specific term, though the general sense is close)

**S-15. (m) Slight addition.** Chinese: 空逈塚間邊際臥坐名阿練若 = "Remote burial grounds and border regions for sitting and lying are called aranyaka." The translation adds "(*aranyaka*)" as a Sanskrit gloss, which is an appropriate scholarly addition rather than a content change. Acceptable.

**S-16. CLEAN.** Five perfections of place: All five are present and correctly translated:
1. 形相端嚴...心悅心喜任持於斷 — beautiful form, clean, gardens/pools, level ground, etc. Accurate.
2. 晝無憒鬧夜少音聲...諸惡毒觸 — quiet by day, little noise by night, few pests. Accurate.
3. 無惡師子虎豹豺狼...安樂而住 — free from dangerous animals, enemies. Accurate.
4. 隨順身命眾具易得...無所匱乏 — necessities easily obtained. Accurate.
5. 有善知識之所攝受...速得清淨 — spiritual friends, wise companions. Accurate.

**S-17. CLEAN.** Deportment section: 謂於晝分經行宴坐...於後夜分疾疾還起經行宴坐 = "During the day, one alternates between walking meditation and sitting in meditation. During the first watch... middle watch... last watch." Accurate mapping of the four time periods.

**S-18. CLEAN.** Five reasons for cross-legged posture: All five correctly rendered:
1. 身攝斂速發輕安 = pliancy arises swiftly
2. 能經久時 = sustained for long time
3. 不共法 = distinctive practice
4. 形相端嚴 = dignified and elegant
5. 佛佛弟子共所開許 = praised by Buddhas and noble ones

**S-19. CLEAN.** 端身正願 section:
- 端身 = 策舉身令其端直 = "making the body upright and erect." Accurate.
- 正願 = 令其心離諂離詐調柔正直 = "making the mind free from deceit and dishonesty -- flexible and upright." Accurate.
- Effects correctly translated: 惛沈睡眠 = "torpor and drowsiness"; 外境散動 = "distraction toward external objects."

**S-20. CLEAN.** Counter-mindfulness (背念) [0450b15]:
- 如理作意相應念名為背念。棄背違逆一切黑品故 = "Mindfulness conjoined with wise attention is called counter-mindfulness -- because it turns its back on and opposes all unwholesome qualities." Accurate.
- 緣定相為境念名為背念。棄背除遣一切不定地所緣境故 = "mindfulness that takes the sign of samadhi as its object is called counter-mindfulness -- because it turns its back on and dispels all objects belonging to the non-concentrated state." Accurate.

**S-21. CLEAN.** Physical seclusion (身遠離): 謂不與在家及出家眾共相雜住。獨一無侶 = "not mingling with householders or monastic communities but dwelling alone, without companions." Accurate.

**S-22. CLEAN.** Mental seclusion (心遠離): 謂遠離一切染污無記所有作意。修習一切其性是善能引義利定地作意及定資糧加行作意 = "abandoning all afflicted and indeterminate mental engagement, and cultivating wholesome attention that leads toward samadhi and its prerequisites -- the attention of the preparatory stage." Accurate.

**S-23. CLEAN.** Summary: 若處所圓滿。若威儀圓滿。若身遠離。若心遠離。總攝為一說名遠離 = "perfection of place, perfection of deportment, physical seclusion, and mental seclusion are gathered together under the single heading of 'seclusion.'" Accurate.

### Section 30.9 — One-Pointed Mind [0450b27]

**S-24. CLEAN.** Core definition: 謂數數隨念同分所緣流注無罪適悅相應令心相續。名三摩地。亦名為善心一境性 = "Repeated mindfulness of a homogeneous object-field, flowing without fault, conjoined with ease, causing the mind to continue -- this is called samadhi, and it is also called wholesome mental one-pointedness." Accurate. Each element of the Chinese definition is present.

**S-25. (m) Minor rendering note — 數數隨念.** Chinese 數數隨念 literally = "again and again following-mindfulness." The translation renders this as "repeated mindfulness," which captures the iterative quality. Acceptable.

**S-26. CLEAN.** Definition of 數數隨念: 謂於正法聽聞受持。從師獲得教誡教授增上力故。令其定地諸相現前。緣此為境。流注無罪適悅相應。所有正念隨轉安住 = "Through hearing and retaining the correct dharma, and through the dominant force of the teacher's instructions and guidance, the characteristics of the concentrated state become present. Taking these as one's object, one's mindfulness turns and abides with ease, free from fault." Accurate.

**S-27. CLEAN.** Definition of 同分所緣: 謂諸定地所緣境界。非一眾多種種品類。緣此為境令心正行。說名為定。此即名為同分所緣 = "The objects of the concentrated state are not a single thing but many and varied. Yet taking these as one's object causes the mind to proceed correctly, and this is called samadhi. Such objects are called 'homogeneous.'" Accurate.

**S-28. CLEAN.** Q&A on 同分: 問此所緣境是誰同分說為同分。答是所知事相似品類故名同分 = "because they belong to the same class of knowable things -- sharing a similar nature." Accurate.

**S-29. (m) Minor note — 熟道.** Chinese: 極安隱住熟道。適悅相應而轉 = "in a state of extreme stability, conjoined with the ease of a well-practiced path." The term 熟道 (mature/well-practiced path) is rendered as "well-practiced path," which is adequate. The translation captures the meaning.

**S-30. CLEAN.** Recapitulation of the full samadhi definition is correctly restated.

**S-31. CLEAN.** Shamatha/vipashyana classification:
- 若於九種心住中心一境性。是名奢摩他品 = "Mental one-pointedness within the nine stages of mental abiding belongs to the shamatha class." Accurate.
- 若於四種慧行中心一境性。是名毘鉢舍那品 = "Mental one-pointedness within the four modes of wisdom-practice belongs to the vipashyana class." Accurate.

**S-32. CLEAN.** 流注適悅相應 and 無罪適悅相應 are correctly distinguished as two separate qualities in the translation: "flowing, conjoined with ease" vs. "without fault, conjoined with ease." The Chinese text explains each separately: 無散亂行無缺無間。無間殷重加行適悅相應而轉 for the first, and 無有染污。極安隱住熟道。適悅相應而轉 for the second. Both are correctly reflected.
