# Applied Register Rewrites: Fascicles 26 and 27

**Source:** `review_findings/final_3A_register_F26_F27.md`
**Date:** 2026-03-02

## Fascicle 26 — Edits Applied

### Five Worst Passages (all applied)

1. **F26-1: Person definitions 3–7 (§26.2)** — Compressed five identical formula definitions into one full definition (greed) plus a consolidated paragraph for aversion/delusion/pride/discursive thought that preserves all content while eliminating formula monotony.

2. **F26-2: Meditation-image passage (§26.5)** — Restructured nested-qualification sentence. The "through X, through Y — Z appears" construction replaced with clearer subject-verb flow: "The meditator does not encounter... What appears is something in between..."

3. **F26-3: Conduct-portrait closing formulas (§26.3)** — Varied or dropped the five identical "Such and similar are to be known as the characteristics of a person of X-conduct" closings. Greed portrait now ends on its final observation; aversion uses "Such is the portrait of"; delusion uses "These are the marks of"; pride drops the formula entirely; discursive thought uses "These are the marks by which... is recognized."

4. **F26-4: Dhyana progression (§26.5)** — Collapsed three repetitive sentences ("For one who enters the first dhyana, at the time of attaining the first dhyana...") into a single principle statement with compressed enumeration.

5. **F26-5: Charnel ground repetitions (§26.6)** — Replaced four "When it is said, 'By the charnel ground path...'" constructions with a single framing sentence followed by integrated quotation-and-gloss pairs. All content preserved.

### Most Urgent Fixes (applied)

- **F26-TE-04: Five-verb chain** (§26.5, image with discernment) — "observe, investigate, thoroughly investigate, comprehensively examine, and comprehensively analyze" collapsed to "examine and probe the object with sustained investigative attention." Same fix applied to two instances in the image-without-discernment passage.
- **F26-TE-03: Triple doublet** (§26.3, delusion portrait) — "Whatever they think, they think it poorly; whatever they say, they say it poorly; whatever they do, they do it poorly" compressed to "They reason badly, speak badly, and act badly."
- **F26-TE-02: Stage-direction phrasing** (§26.3, greed portrait) — "With relaxed countenance, level gaze, and smiling face, they speak first to others" rewritten as "Their face is open and smiling; they greet others readily and speak first."
- **F26-FM-03: Establishment formula x11** (§26.3) — Varied the closing formula for criteria 7–11: "This completes..." / "This covers..." replacing the uniform "Such is what is called the establishment of persons by differentiation of X."

## Fascicle 27 — Edits Applied

### Five Worst Passages (all applied)

1. **F27-1: Element-definition formula (§27.2)** — Preserved full template for earth element. Compressed water into a single paragraph with the twofold pattern noted. Restructured fire and wind as bold-headed paragraphs eliminating the "included in the category of X, included in the category of Y" formula. All instance lists and footnotes preserved.

2. **F27-2: Reverse counting (§27.3)** — Replaced full digit-by-digit expansion ("then nine, then eight, then seven...") with "counts backward from ten to one."

3. **F27-3: Three counter-examples for breath (§27.3)** — Restructured three nested conditional arguments into cleaner if/then format with explicit "but they do not" conclusions. All counter-examples and embryonic-stage terms preserved.

4. **F27-4: Dependent origination chain (§27.4)** — Replaced nested participial construction with sequential sentences. Compressed "grief, withering, sorrow, lamentation, anguish, suffering, and distress" to "the full range of suffering from grief and lamentation through anguish and distress." Core doctrinal content (impermanence → suffering → non-self) preserved.

5. **F27-5: Suffering of conditioned existence (§27.5)** — Broke seven-participial sentence into sequential short sentences. Main predicate ("they are said to be 'suffering'") no longer buried. All seven qualities preserved as separate clauses.

### Most Urgent Fixes (applied)

- **F27-TE-03: Advanced counting doubled subject** — "this... this is called" replaced with "when the mind remains undistracted throughout this... one is said to have become well-practiced."
- **F27-FM-02: "collectively called the X skandha" x5** — Varied last three instances: perception uses "constitutes," formations uses "constitute," consciousness uses "These together are."
- **F27-NQ-03: Elements skill definition** — Restructured "If one truly knows... its own distinct element, its own distinct seed, its own distinct lineage" into clearer "Skill in the elements means knowing that these eighteen dharmas each arise from their own distinct element, seed, and lineage."
