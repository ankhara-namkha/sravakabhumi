# Fascicle 30 Structural Breakdown

**Division:** 本地分中聲聞地第十三第三瑜伽處之一 (Sravakabhumi, Third Yoga Place, Part One)

---

## Summary Verse (Uddana)

[0448b25] provides the mnemonic verse (總嗢柁南) that outlines the entire Third Yoga Place:

> 往慶問尋求　　方安立護養
> 出離一境性　　障淨修作意

The ten topics encoded in this verse:
1. 往 — Approaching [the teacher]
2. 慶 — Encouragement/commendation
3. 問 — Four inquiry points (審問)
4. 尋求 — Four-fold investigation (of aspiration, lineage, faculties, disposition)
5. 方安立 — Establishment in five places
6. 護養 — Guarding/nurturing samadhi resources
7. 出離 — Seclusion/renunciation
8. 一境性 — One-pointed mind (samadhi)
9. 障淨 — Purification of obstacles
10. 修作意 — Cultivating attention/mental engagement

## Structural Summary

The fascicle divides into these major blocks:

1. **Preamble and Uddana** [0448b25] — Transition verse listing all ten topics of the Third Yoga Place
2. **Approaching the Teacher** [0448c01-0449a09] — Student's protocol and attitudes
3. **Teacher's Encouragement** [0448c13-0449a09] — Eight declarations of praise
4. **Four Inquiry Points** [0449a10-0449a16] — Preliminary examination
5. **Four-fold Investigation** [0449a17-0449c14] — Aspiration, lineage, faculties, disposition (the largest single block in terms of methods)
6. **Five Establishments — Overview** [0449c15]
7. **First Establishment: Guarding Samadhi Resources** [0449c15-0450a02]
8. **Second Establishment: Seclusion** [0450a03-0450b26] — Place, posture, body/mind seclusion
9. **Third Establishment: One-Pointed Mind** [0450b27-0450c17] — Definition of samadhi
10. **Nine Stages of Mental Abiding** [0450c18-0451b12] — With six powers and four types of attention
11. **Four Types of Vipashyana** [0451b13-0452a10] — Four operations, three doors, six aspects, three understandings
12. **Vipashyana Applied to Impurity** [0452a11-0453a28] — Full six-aspect analysis
13. **Vipashyana Applied to Loving-Kindness** [0453a29-end] — Full six-aspect analysis

**Note:** The fascicle covers topics 1-5 and the beginning of 6-8 from the uddana outline. Topics 9 (障淨, purification of obstacles) and 10 (修作意, cultivating attention) are NOT covered in fascicle 30 — they presumably continue in fascicle 31. The fourth establishment (障清淨) and fifth establishment (修作意) are named in the five-establishment overview at [0449c15] but not elaborated in this fascicle.
